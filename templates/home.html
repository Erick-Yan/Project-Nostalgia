"We Are All Pieces Of What We Remember..."

- Cassandra Clare

In a time where the existence of social media has taken over our hectic lives, memorable moments have often become subject to nothing more than for likes and shares. While a quick post can satisfy you for the day, we believe that the lost art of journaling can last you a lifetime. By bringing the journaling experience online, you will be able to cherish and save all your memories within our Timeline. We will also be optimizing your journals through music, a potent stimulator for nostalgia. Through our online application, we hope to curate the most personalized experience for users to create and reminisce. Welcome to Project Nostalgia.
